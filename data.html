Step 1 — The Data

Who are our customers?

Our bank serves 10,000 customers across three European countries. Before we can act on customer retention, we first need to understand who our customers are and what information we track about them.

Executive summary

The bank is losing 1 in 5 customers every year, with women aged 50–59 churning at nearly 63%. We built an AI model that predicts who will leave with 87% accuracy — giving retention teams a focused, weekly action list instead of blanket campaigns.
